ATTRACTION IN BANDAR BARU, KAMPAR.

Explore the breathtaking beauty and thrilling adventures that await you in Bandar Baru Kampar.
From serene nature spots to exciting activities, we offer something for everyone.

Nature

Red-Eared Sliders

These fascinating turtles are commonly found in our natural lakes, offering an interesting view for nature lovers.

Adventure

Jogging Track

A scenic track for runners and fitness enthusiasts, offering both challenges and beautiful views along the way.

Bandar Baru Kampar is a thriving township in Perak, Malaysia, combining modern amenities, rich history, and picturesque natural surroundings.

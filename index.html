ACTIVITIES TO DO:

in Bandar Baru Kampar

01

Serene Escapes

Unwind in the tranquil parks and scenic lakes of Bandar Baru Kampar.... Perfect for jogging, walking, or simply soaking in nature, these serene spots offer a refreshing escape from the busy town life. Whether you're looking to relax by the water or enjoy a morning run, the peaceful surroundings are sure to rejuvenate your mind and body.

02

Historical Insights

Step back in time and explore the fascinating history of Kampar at the Tin Mining Museum.... This cultural gem showcases exhibits on the town's tin-mining heritage, including vintage equipment, historical photos, and stories of miners' lives. It's an enriching experience that brings local history to life.

03

Bowling Fun

Gather your friends and family for a lively game at the bowling alley!... Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned bowler, the fun atmosphere and modern facilities guarantee a great time. It’s the perfect activity for bonding, friendly competitions, or simply having a blast indoors.

04

Cinema Experience

Enjoy the latest movies in a comfortable and modern cinema right in Bandar Baru Kampar.... Perfect for a relaxing outing with family, friends, or a solo escape, the cinema offers a wide selection of films to suit all tastes. Sit back, grab some popcorn, and immerse yourself in the magic of the big screen.

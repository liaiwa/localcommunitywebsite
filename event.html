EVENT

Ramadan Bazaar

The Ramadan Bazaar in Bandar Baru, Kampar is a vibrant market typically held during the holy month of Ramadan, where people gather to enjoy a variety of food, goods, and cultural experiences. The bazaar is very popular, featuring an array of stalls selling traditional foods, sweet treats, clothing, and decorative items relevant to the festive season. The atmosphere is lively, filled with communal spirit as families and friends come together to break their fast, shop, and partake in the night activities that often include entertainment and socializing. Ramadan Bazaars not only serve as a focal point for Eid preparations but also foster a sense of community and celebration among attendees.

Thai Food Festival

The Thai Food Festival in Bandar Baru, Kampar is a culinary event that celebrates the rich flavors and vibrant cuisine of Thailand. The festival features an array of authentic Thai dishes, including spicy salads, aromatic curries, and flavorful street food, often prepared by renowned Thai chefs. In addition to savoring delicious meals, attendees can enjoy cultural performances, cooking demonstrations, and workshops that showcase Thai culinary traditions. The festival provides a unique opportunity for food enthusiasts to indulge in the diverse tastes of Thailand while fostering cultural exchange and appreciation between Malaysian and Thai communities.

Geopark Fun Run

The Geopark Fun Run in Bandar Baru, Kampar, is an exciting outdoor event that invites participants of all ages to explore the stunning natural beauty and geological features of the area while promoting health and fitness. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant geopark, the event typically includes various distances to cater to runners of different skill levels, fostering a sense of community and environmental awareness. Local attractions and cultural experiences often complement the run, making it a festive occasion that highlights the uniqueness of the Kampar region.

Mid-Autumn Festival

During the Mid-Autumn Festival at Lake View in Bandar Baru, Kampar, the atmosphere comes alive with vibrant celebrations as families gather to admire the glowing full moon reflecting on the serene waters. Colorful lanterns illuminate the lakeside, casting a warm, festive glow while locals indulge in traditional mooncakes and enjoy cultural performances. The combination of natural beauty and joyful festivities creates a magical ambiance, making it a perfect time for social gatherings and memorable moments amidst the picturesque landscape.
